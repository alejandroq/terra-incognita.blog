constraints on possibility

In an intuitive sense, freedom is collated with endless grass fields of creative plight. After all what is to interrupt radiating creative disposition in such a setting? No man or external force to block you way. However in observation and consideration - expression is the swash buckling pirate against tirades of constraint. Is is the shadow that parallels the light, but never quite meeting. Creative or innovative processes are surely sovereign but outputs as would a black hole’s event horizon.
